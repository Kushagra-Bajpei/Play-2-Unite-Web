About Us

Play 2 Unite is the premier events club at our college, bringing together students who want to create memorable experiences and build lasting connections. Founded in 2018, we've grown to become one of the largest and most active clubs on campus.

Our mission is to create an inclusive community where students can connect through diverse events - from technical workshops and gaming tournaments to cultural festivals and social gatherings. We believe in the power of events to unite people from all backgrounds.

Whether you're interested in organizing events, participating in activities, or just looking to meet new people, Play 2 Unite is your platform for creating memorable college experiences. We believe in the power of events to build friendships and develop valuable skills.
